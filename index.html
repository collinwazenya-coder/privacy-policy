Privacy Policy for Smart Health

Last updated: June 9, 2026

1. Introduction Welcome to Smart Health ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. 2. Information We Collect We collect the following types of information: a) Personal Information For Patients: Name, email address, age, gender, blood group, patient ID, medical history (vitals, prescriptions, appointments). For Doctors: Name, email address, hospital name, department, passkey (generated by hospital admin), and activity logs (login, patient registrations, prescriptions, vitals, appointment approvals/rejections). For Hospital Administrators: Email address, password (hashed), hospital name, list of generated doctor passkeys. b) Health Data Vitals (blood pressure, heart rate, temperature, SpO₂) Prescriptions (medication, dosage, frequency, duration, instructions) Appointment details (date, time, reason, status, doctor assigned) c) Device and Usage Data Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers. App usage statistics (e.g., feature access, time spent). d) Location Data Precise location (GPS) is accessed only when you activate the SOS Emergency feature. We do not track location at any other time. Location is used to share your coordinates with emergency services or contacts. e) Emergency Call Data When you use SOS, the app may open your device’s dialer with the emergency number (e.g., 911). We do not record or store call audio or the fact that a call was made. 3. How We Use Your Information We use the collected information for the following purposes: Service Delivery: Manage patient registration, doctor‑patient relationships, appointment booking, prescription writing, and vitals recording. Emergency Response: Use your location during SOS activation to assist emergency services. Communication: Send appointment reminders, prescription updates, and health alerts (via notifications). Improvement: Analyze usage trends to enhance app functionality and user experience. Security: Authenticate users (passkey for doctors, email/password for patients and hospital admins) and prevent unauthorised access. Compliance: Fulfill legal obligations (e.g., respond to lawful requests). 4. How We Share Your Information We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information only in the following circumstances: With your consent: For example, sharing your location with emergency services during SOS. With healthcare providers: Doctors can view patients they have registered or approved. Hospital administrators can view all doctors and aggregated activity reports. As required by law: To comply with a subpoena, court order, or other legal process. Service providers: We may use third‑party libraries (e.g., SharedPreferences, permission_handler, geolocator) that operate locally on your device; they do not transmit data to external servers. 5. Data Storage and Security Local Storage: All data (passkeys, patients, prescriptions, vitals, appointments, doctor‑patient relations, activities) is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. We do not upload any data to external cloud servers unless you explicitly integrate such services. Security Measures: We implement reasonable technical safeguards (e.g., encrypting sensitive data where possible). However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your device and login credentials safe. Retention: Data is retained until you delete the app or manually clear app data. Deleted data may remain in the device’s backup systems for a limited time. 6. Your Rights Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to: Access your personal data. Correct inaccurate information. Delete your data (uninstall the app or use the app’s delete functions). Withdraw consent for location or notifications (via device settings). Data portability (request a copy of your data). To exercise these rights, contact us at collinwazenya@gmail.com 7. Children’s Privacy The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App and the “Last updated” date will be revised. Your continued use of the App after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy. 9.
