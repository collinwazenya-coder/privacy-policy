Request Account Deletion for Smart Health To ensure the security of your account and data, we require a verifiable request. Please follow the steps below to permanently delete your Smart Health account and all associated data. Option 1: Delete from the Smart Health Mobile App (Recommended) This is the most secure and straightforward method. The app already contains a feature for this purpose. Open the Smart Health app on your device. Tap the menu icon (☰) in the top-left corner to open the navigation drawer. Scroll down and tap on "Request Account Deletion". Complete the short form to confirm your email address and submit your request. Your request will be sent to our admin team for processing. Option 2: Send an Email Request If you no longer have the app installed, you can also request deletion via email. Send an email from your registered email address to our admin team at: collinwazenya@gmail.com Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request - Smart Health In the email body, please include your full name and the email address associated with your Smart Health account. Once we receive your email, our team will verify your identity and process the deletion within 14 days. Note: All data stored within the Smart Health app (vitals, prescriptions, appointments) is stored locally on your device and will be permanently removed when you uninstall the app. The deletion request above ensures your account is also removed from our admin records. If you need assistance, please contact our support team at collinwazenya@gmail.com.
