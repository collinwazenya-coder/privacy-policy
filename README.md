**A smart health assistant application for patient monitoring and clinical management**

**PROBLEM STATEMENT**
Limited access to timely and reliable healthcare services, coupled with poor appointment management and weak patient-doctor communication, continues to reduce the efficiency of healthcare delivery. 
Existing methods such as manual booking, phone calls, and paper-based records are inefficient, error-prone, and lack real-time coordination. 
As a result, patients experience long waiting times, missed appointments, and delayed treatment, while healthcare providers struggle to manage patient schedules effectively, leading to overcrowding, reduced patient satisfaction, and poor health outcomes.

Smart Health is a comprehensive healthcare management app that connects patients,doctors, and hospital administrators in one secure platform.



Features

For Patients
- **Health Dashboard** – View your vitals, prescriptions, and appointments in one place
- **Book Appointments** – Schedule appointments with doctors by department
- **Vitals History** – Track blood pressure, heart rate, temperature, and SpO₂ over time
- **Medication Management** – View all your prescriptions and their status
- **SOS Emergency** – One‑tap emergency alert that shares your location and calls emergency services
- **Account Deletion** – Request account deletion directly from the app

 For Doctors
- **Patient Management** – Register new patients, view patient history, and manage records
- **Vitals Recording** – Record and track patient vitals
- **Prescription Writing** – Write and manage prescriptions for your patients
- **Appointment Management** – Accept or reject patient appointment requests
- **Activity Logging** – All actions are tracked for hospital reporting

For Hospital Administrators
- **Doctor Passkey Management** – Generate, activate, and revoke doctor passkeys
- **Doctor Management** – View all registered doctors with their departments
- **Activity Reports** – View weekly, monthly, and annual doctor activity reports
- **PDF Export** – Export reports as PDF files

 🚀 How to Use

1. Hospital Admin (First Setup)

1. **Sign Up** – Open the app and select **"Hospital Administration"**.
2. Create a hospital account with:
   - Hospital Email
   - Strong Password
   - Hospital Name
3. **Log in** with your email and password.
4. **Generate doctor passkeys**:
   - Go to the **Passkeys** tab
   - Tap **"Generate Passkey"**
   - Enter:
     - Doctor's Full Name
     - Doctor's Email (valid format)
     - Department (e.g., Cardiology, Pediatrics)
     - Hospital Name
   - The passkey (e.g., `DOC-123456789`) will be generated automatically.
5. **Share the passkey** with the doctor along with their email.

 2. Doctor Login

1. Open the app and select **"Doctor Portal"**.
2. Enter:
   - **Work Email** – the email the hospital admin used when generating the passkey
   - **Doctor Passkey** – the passkey generated by the hospital admin
3. Tap **"Access Doctor Portal"**.
4. You will see your **Doctor Dashboard** with:
   - **Overview** – statistics and recent activity
   - **Vitals** – record patient vitals
   - **Prescriptions** – write and manage prescriptions
   - **Appointments** – view and respond to patient bookings
   - **Patients** – register and manage your patients

 3. Patient Login

1. **Get registered** – A doctor must register you as a patient first (or the hospital admin).
2. Open the app and select **"Patient Portal"**.
3. Enter your **Email** and **Password** (set up on first login).
4. Tap **"Sign In to Patient Portal"**.
5. You will see your **Patient Dashboard** with:
   - **Overview** – health summary and vitals
   - **Vitals** – view your recorded vitals
   - **Medications** – view active prescriptions
   - **Appointments** – book new appointments with doctors

Security Features

- **Passkey Authentication** – Doctors log in using secure, hospital-issued passkeys
- **Local Data Storage** – All data stays on your device – nothing is uploaded to external servers
- **Password Management** – Patients create strong passwords on first login (8+ chars, uppercase, lowercase, number, special character)
- **Role‑Based Access** – Each user type sees only what they are authorised to see

## 🆘 Emergency SOS

The SOS feature is available to **all users**, even before registration:

1. Tap the **SOS EMERGENCY** button (red button at the bottom).
2. Confirm activation.
3. The app will:
   - Share your real‑time GPS location
   - Open the emergency dialer (e.g., 911)
   - Alert hospital staff (if registered)

> **Note:** Location permission is required for SOS.
